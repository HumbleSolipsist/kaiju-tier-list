Andrew's Kaiju Tier List

My kaiju film tier list. The term "kaiju" is used loosely here. There are many items in here whose placement I am uncertain of, but I think the list is mostly well organised. There is a little blurb for each rated film which you can see by hovering over the film's entry with your mouse (doesn't work on mobile). The fact that all the S-tier films were released after my birth is a coincidence, as I am immune to bias and nostalgia.
